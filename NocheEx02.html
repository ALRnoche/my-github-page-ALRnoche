Autobiography

André Luis R. Noche is a BS Computer Science student who prefers to be called as Jaco.
Also known as "PsychoticMaker" and "Artist2tell," Jaco's passion for art evolved into profound love for UI design, Game Development, and Graphic Design.
Inspired by the Japanese King of Horror, Junji Ito, and Tim Burton's surrealistic universe, Jaco has emerged as a innovative artist, captivating his audiences and fellow artists with his signature style.
To this day, Jaco is an isko from the University Of The Philippines Los Baños, a proud member in UPLB Computer Science Society, and a guild member in The Graphic Literature Guild.
